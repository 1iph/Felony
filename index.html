About Us

Who We Are

Felony is a bedwars 4v4 guild comprised of dedicated and skilled players who have come together to create a supportive and competitive gaming community. Our members are united by their passion for gaming excellence and collaborative spirit.

We participate in various Hypixel game modes and competitions, consistently ranking among the top performers. Beyond competition, we value camaraderie and friendship, making our guild not just a team but a family of like-minded gamers.

Whether you're looking to improve your skills, participate in guild events, or simply find a welcoming community of players, Felony offers a home for passionate gamers who strive for excellence.

Our History

Founding of Felony

11/30/20

Felony was established by a small group of dedicated 4v4 players.

First Major Tournament Win

April 2020

Our guild secured its first major tournament victory, establishing our presence among the top-tier Hypixel guilds.

Guild Expansion

August 2020

Following our early successes, Felony expanded its membership and implemented a structured ranking system to accommodate growth.

Community Outreach Program

January 2021

We launched our community outreach program, hosting events for new Hypixel players and establishing a mentorship system.

Championship Season

June 2021

Felony dominated the summer championship season, securing victories in multiple game modes and solidifying our reputation.

Present Day

2025

Today, Felony continues to grow and evolve, maintaining our position as one of Hypixel's premier guilds while fostering a supportive community.
